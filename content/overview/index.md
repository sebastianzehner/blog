## Reading Mode

In the top menu you'll find an accessibility icon where you can adjust the following settings:

- **Disable blur** – removes the blur effect in the background
- **Disable background image** – hides background images
- **Show link underline** – makes links more recognizable through underlining
- **Enable Zen Mode** – hides distractions and focuses the view on the article text
- **Font size** – adjusts the font size across the entire blog

The **Zen Mode** can also be activated directly while reading an article via this icon:
